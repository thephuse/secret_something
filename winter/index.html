Secret Santa

This app is designed to help automate your Secret Santa gift exchange with friends or co-workers. Before getting started, please have a list of all participants' names and email addresses handy. After filling in the information on the following screens, your participants will be notified on the specified match date who they will be buying for (matches are assigned randomly) and when the gift exchange will take place.
