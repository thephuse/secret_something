Secret Santa

Need a quick, modern way to organize a Secret Santa or similar gift exchange for your holiday celebrations? You've come to the right place!

Phuse made this simple app to help automate the process for your family, friends or remote team. Find, use, or contribute to the code on GitHub.

Here’s what you need:

  • A group of amazing people you love.
  • The full name and email address of each person.
  • A way to distribute the gifts. If physical mailing addresses are needed, distribute those privately (one mass email will do the trick!)
  • A date your exchange will take place (i.e. Dec 24th).

After filling in your information on the screens that follow, participants will be notified by email about their match and the date of the event!

Technical note: This app doesn’t store any data. Emails are sent via Mandrill.

Happy gifting!
